  • WaveWear Renewal

    It is a platform specialized in providing resources to the community to independently carry out repairs and alterations. Additionally, it offers a space to connect the community with professionals specialized in performing alterations and repairs on garments. In this way, it promotes the circular economy, supports the conservation of our ecosystem, and contributes to fostering the local economy.
